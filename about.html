About me

Hello, my name is Liezzy Mihalko. I am honored to have you interested in knowing more about me and incredibly thrilled to have the opportunity to share a little bit about myself. While I am relatively new to the world of Front-End Engineering, my passion to express my creativity through technology and my determination to learn and grow in this field have been my motives in this journey. From a young age, I found myself drawn to computers and fascinated by the possibilities they offer. This curiosity and enthusiasm have propelled me to pursue a career in Software Engineering. Although I may lack formal experience, I am a dedicated learner and have invested countless hours in self-study gaining my certifications through completed workshops in "SheCodes" and online resources to build a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Responsive Web Design, and React. I am confident in my ability to quickly grasp new concepts and apply them effectively. One aspect that excites me about Front-End Engineering is the ability to bring design concepts to life. I believe that a beautiful and intuitive user interface is crucial for creating a positive user experience. With my eye for aesthetics and attention to detail, I am committed to creating visually appealing and functional websites. Moreover, I understand the importance of collaboration in the software development process. As a team player, I value effective communication and thrive in an environment where different perspectives come together to create innovative solutions. I am always eager to learn from experienced professionals and contribute my ideas to the team.

In addition to technical skills, I am also a strong advocate for user-centered design. I believe in putting the end-users at the forefront of the development process and crafting interfaces that meet their needs and expectations. Empathy and usability are key principles that guide my approach to front-end engineering. Continuous learning is a fundamental aspect of front-end engineering, and I embrace this mindset wholeheartedly. I stay updated with the latest industry trends, attend webinars and conferences, and actively engage in online communities to expand my knowledge and stay ahead in this ever-evolving field. Beyond the technical realm, I possess excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to think critically. I approach challenges with a positive attitude and a determination to find creative solutions. I believe that perseverance and adaptability are essential traits for success in the rapidly changing landscape of technology. Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy exploring creative outlets such as photography and graphic design. These hobbies have nurtured my creativity and have taught me to approach problem-solving from different angles. I find inspiration in the world around me and seek to bring that inspiration into my work as a front-end engineer. In conclusion, while I may be a front-end engineer with limited professional experience, I am passionate, dedicated, and eager to contribute to the field. I am committed to continuously honing my skills, collaborating with others, and creating meaningful and delightful user experiences. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to making a positive impact as a front-end engineer.
