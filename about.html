About me

Hello, my name is Liezzy Mihalko. I am honored to have you interested in knowing more about me and incredibly thrilled to have the opportunity to share a little bit about myself. While I am relatively new to the world of Front-End Engineering, my passion to express my creativity through technology and my determination to learn and grow in this field have kept me motivated in this journey. From a young age, I found myself drawn to computers and fascinated by the possibilities they offer. My curiosity and enthusiasm have propelled me to pursue a career in Software Engineering. Although I may lack formal experience, I am a dedicated learner and have invested countless hours in self-study gaining my certifications through completed workshops in "SheCodes" and online resources to build a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Responsive Web Design, React, and working on real world projects. I am confident in my ability to quickly grasp new concepts and apply them effectively. One aspect that excites me about Front-End Engineering is the ability to bring design concepts to life. With my eye for aesthetics and attention to detail, I am committed to creating visually appealing and functional websites. Moreover, I understand the importance of collaboration in the software development process. As a team player, I value effective communication and thrive in an environment where different perspectives come together to create innovative solutions. I am always eager to learn from experienced professionals and contribute my ideas to the team.

In addition, I know continuous learning is a fundamental aspect of Front-end Engineering, and I embrace this mindset wholeheartedly; that is one of many reasons why I chose this career path. I stay updated with the latest industry trends, attend webinars and conferences, and actively engage in online communities to expand my knowledge and stay ahead in this ever-evolving field. Beyond the technical realm, I possess excellent problem-solving skills, the ability to communicate effectively, and think critically. I approach challenges with a positive attitude and a determination to find creative solutions. I believe that perseverance and adaptability are essential traits for success in the rapidly changing landscape of technology. Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy exploring creative outlets such as photography and illustration art. These hobbies have nurtured my creativity and have taught me to approach problem-solving from different angles while trusting the process of a new project. I find inspiration in the world around me and seek to bring that inspiration into my work. In conclusion, while I may be a front-end engineer with limited professional experience, I am passionate, dedicated, and eager to contribute to the field. I am committed to continuously honing my skills, collaborating with others, and creating meaningful and delightful user experiences. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to making a positive impact as a Front-End Engineer.
